
Power BI Integration Steps:
1. Open Power BI Desktop.
2. Choose 'Get Data' > 'SQL Database'. For local testing, use 'SQLite', or 'CSV' as needed.
3. Connect to vaccination.db (if using SQLite: install SQLite connector for Power BI).
4. Import tables: coverage_data, incidence_data, reported_cases, vaccine_intro, vaccine_schedule.
5. Sample DAX: For filtering KPIs, trend lines, and heatmaps
6. Use slicers for year, country, antigen, disease
7. For geographical heatmaps: use country_name + Coverage or incidence
